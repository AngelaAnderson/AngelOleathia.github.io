Hi, My name is Angel...

I’m an accountant turned marketer who blossomed into an empathetic UX/UI Designer. I love eating, listening to podcasts, being around animals and -- Oh yeah -- I’m also a Navy wife married to a devoted sneakerhead from Chicago, IL. That last part may seem like an extremely random and unnecessary fact about me, but I promise it’s not! :)

In all actuality, my husband’s obsession with sneakers helped me conceptualize my goals of design. His love for sneakers, especially the Jordan XX’s, stems from his desire to feel good about himself. Everbody wants to be ‘Like Mike’ and to be like Mike made him feel like he had superpowers. It gave him hope. Confidence. Motivation. Passion! And that passion, that love, is infectious. So much so that it’s helped me come to understand design as more than just a tool to solve problems.

As I mentioned previously, I’m an empathetic person. I’m not emotional, just empathetic. Basically what I mean is that I live for emotionally enticing experiences. I enjoy that rush of excitement I feel every time I’m inspired or motivated. Feelings are enticing forces that can seek out and create value, and as a young new designer that’s my ultimate goal! I seek to create added value through building experiences and communicating such value through design and visually immersive experiences.
